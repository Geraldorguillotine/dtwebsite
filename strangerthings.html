after playing dungeons and dragons with his friend's mike wheeler, Dustin Henderson, and Lucas Sinclair, will byers vanish while cycling home. Will's Joyce byers reports the disappearance to police chief Jim hopper. Eleven as young girl with telekinesis and telepathic abilities who escaped a laboratory run by Dr martin Brenner. While searching for will mike, Dustin and Lucas come across eleven. The boys bring eleven to mike's basement and mike begins to catch feelings. Eleven indicates that will is on a place called the upside down a place where mike's sister Nancy's friend Barbara vanishes and dies after being attacked by a creature called the Demogorgon. Joyce tries to locate will with Christmas lights and it turns out will is alive can communicate through the lights. The body of will turns up in a quarry but it turns out to be fake and planted by the lab. Hopper breaks into the lab and gets knocked out. The boys along with eleven trying to find will, searches in the forest. Nancy and will's brother Jonathan plans to kill the Demogorgon but fails. The lab agents are searching for eleven and they all flee the house and escape them. To find will, the group asks eleven to search for them telepathically. To do this they make a makeshift sensory deprivation tank to amplify eleven's powers. She finds will hiding in the upside-down version of his backyard fort. Realizing that the gate to the upside down is at the basement of the lab, hopper and Joyce breaks into the lab but gets apprehended by guards. In exchange for eleven's location brener allows hopper and Joyce to enter the upside down to rescue will. The Demogorgon arrives at the byer's house and Jonathan, Nancy along with his boyfriend Steve fights it off forcing it to retreat back to the upside down. The lab agents arrive to get eleven but eleven kills most of the agents before collapsing from exhaustion. The Demogorgon arrives after being attracted by the blood of the corpses and attacks the group. Eleven wakes up and disintegrates the Demogorgon. Hopper and Joyce rescue and revives will byers and will recovers in the hospital. At a family dinner, will coughs up a slug like creature from the upside down
