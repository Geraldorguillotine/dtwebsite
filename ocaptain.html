O CAPTAIN MY CAPTAIN is a poem written by walt Whitman in 1865. The poem is about the assassination of Abraham Lincoln, the us president. it was published on November 4 1865 and became the most popular poem by walt Whitman. Although never meeting Lincoln in person, Whitman felt a connection to him and was moved by his assassination. This poem is a metaphor of a ship's captain who has passed away to allude to President Abraham Lincoln's passing at the conclusion of the American Civil War. Grief over the shocking loss of the president can be expressed by mourning the death of the captain.
