Jung yoo jung a 23-year-old woman in Busan confessed to the premeditated murder of an English tutor. Jung yoo jung posed as a mother of a school child wanting her daughter to learn English. Then jung yoo jung pretended to be the daughter by wearing school uniform then entered the victim's house. She then murdered and dismembered her victim and placed the body parts in a suitcase. She then entered a taxi to go to a wooded area. the taxi driver however became quite suspicious because of the blood seeping through the suitcase and alerted authorities. Jung was taken into custody and she stated that she was really sorry to the victim's family. The motive of this act was that jung was a crime story fanatic that was obsessed with crime tv shows and books
